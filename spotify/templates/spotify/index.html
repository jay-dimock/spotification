Welcome to Spotification!

This site connects to your personal Spotify account and allows you to group your playlists. You may then play groups of playlists together. For example, you might choose put your Latin Dance, HipHop Dance, and Electronic Dance playlists all under a "Dance" group. Playing that group would mix all of its playlists together.

What would you like to do?

  • Manage Playlists
  • Manage Groups
  • Play a Playlist or Group - in a new tab
  • Play a Playlist or Group - in this tab
