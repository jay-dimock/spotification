Welcome to Spotification!

This site connects to your personal Spotify account and allows you to group your playlists. You may then play groups of playlists together.

For example, you might create a group called "Dance" which contains these playlists: Latin Dance, HipHop Dance, and Electronic Dance. When you play the Dance group, the player will shuffle the tracks together from those playslists and play them for you.

What would you like to do?

  • Manage Playlists
  • Manage Groups
  • Play a Playlist or Group - in a new tab
  • Play a Playlist or Group - in this tab
